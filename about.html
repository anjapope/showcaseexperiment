Our Mission

We are a multidisciplinary research team dedicated to understanding ivory materials through comprehensive investigation. Our work combines cutting-edge scientific analysis with the rich context provided by oral histories and archival research.

By integrating these approaches, we aim to:

  • Determine the origin, age, and authenticity of ivory objects
  • Preserve knowledge about ivory traditions and craftsmanship
  • Support conservation efforts and legal enforcement
  • Contribute to the broader understanding of cultural heritage
